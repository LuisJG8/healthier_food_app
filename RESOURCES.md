# Strategic Programming Resources

## Knowledge

- [Google Engineering Practices: Small CLs](https://google.github.io/eng-practices/review/developer/small-cls.html)
  Practical guide to decomposing work into reviewable changes. Use for: learning to plan slices, separate refactors from behavior changes, and avoid large unreviewable AI-generated patches.
- [DORA Capability Catalog](https://dora.dev/capabilities/)
  Research-backed catalog of delivery capabilities, including maintainability, customer feedback, test automation, small batches, and visibility of work. Use for: connecting codebase habits to delivery outcomes.
- [John Ousterhout: _A Philosophy of Software Design_](https://web.stanford.edu/~ouster/cgi-bin/aposd.php)
  Software design reference centered on managing complexity and deciding what matters. Use for: architecture discussions, abstraction choices, and codebase design judgment.
- [StaffEng: Staff Archetypes](https://staffeng.com/guides/staff-archetypes/)
  Useful taxonomy of senior technical leadership modes: tech lead, architect, solver, and right hand. Use for: understanding the non-coding shapes of senior engineering work.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [DORA Community](https://dora.community/)
  Practitioner community around software delivery performance. Use for: comparing delivery practices and learning how experienced teams operationalize feedback loops.
- [StaffEng Stories](https://staffeng.com/stories/)
  Interviews with senior individual contributors. Use for: seeing how technical strategy, influence, execution, and organizational judgment show up in real careers.
